FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Let us help answer the most common questions you might have.

I am working on an open source project. Can I get free access to Akedo?

Absolutely. Contact us and we'll get you setup so that you can start ideating in Akedo. Because you'll have access to the Launchers plan, you can publish a public roadmap and change log directly within Akedo to share with your users.

Do I need a credit card to try Akedo out?

No, you can create unlimited teams on the Individual plan at no cost. If you'd like to start inviting other team members, among other things, you'll need to upgrade your team and that will require a credit card.

What if I need a longer trial?

All our plans come wiht a 30 day free trial. Things happen, we get it, and sometimes that's not enough time. Get in touch and we'd be happy to extend your trial.

Can Akedo integration with any tools?

Yes. Akedo currently integrates with Github and Slack with plans to add new integrations in the near future. If you'd like to suggest an integration, head on over to our public roadmap and submit your idea!

How do startups qualify for access to Akedo?

If you're a new startup that hasn't yet launched and don't have any customers, you qualify for Akedo. Contact us and we'll get you started with an Akedo account.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancel anytime. No questions asked, no harrassement and no penalty. If you've prepaid for a year, we'll refund any unused months.
